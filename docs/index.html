ScanHisaab

Scan paper receipts and track your expenses, fully on-device.

ScanHisaab turns your phone's camera into a receipt scanner. Snap a photo of any paper receipt and the app extracts the merchant name, date, and total amount using Google ML Kit's on-device text recognition. All your scanned receipts live on your phone - nothing is uploaded to a server or shared with anyone.

What it does

  • Scan paper receipts with your phone's camera
  • Read merchant, date, and total automatically via on-device OCR
  • Track monthly spending and stay under a budget
  • View, edit, and delete past receipts
  • Get local budget-alert notifications

Privacy first

The whole point of ScanHisaab is that your receipts stay yours. The app:

  • Has no account or login
  • Sends zero data to any server (no analytics, no cloud sync, no telemetry)
  • Processes images entirely on your device
  • Stores everything in Android's standard app storage; uninstalling removes everything
